Selected work

SynthSite — Synthetic video generation & benchmarking

SynthSite began as a practical deployment problem — evaluating safety site-analytics vendors with almost no footage of rare hazards — and grew into research. It is a hybrid local–cloud video generation pipeline with human curation and safety annotation, LoRA fine-tuned to synthesize rare worker-under-suspended-load scenarios, with agency domain experts reviewing the outputs. Published at the CVPR 2026 SynData4CV workshop; dataset on Hugging Face; extended into the ACM Multimedia Dataset Track.

Video diffusion · Wan2.2-I2V-A14B · Qwen2.5-VL curation · LoRA · 227 rare scenarios · VBench · open-vocabulary detection

DataSharingAssist → Privacy Scientist

I ideated and product-led DataSharingAssist, an AI assistant for explainable data classification and data-sharing guidance — recommendations and justifications grounded in government policies through retrieval, with the full reasoning process visible to the user. It gained traction with 20+ agencies through in-person pilots with data teams and policymakers, was presented at the IEEE Symposium on Privacy Expectations in New York, and was selected into the government-wide Build for Good hackathon for its high impact.

I am now evolving it into Privacy Scientist: a multimodal, agentic system pairing document intelligence — OCR, vision, and language understanding — with orchestrated agents for policy resolution, mitigation search, evaluation, and red-teaming, optimizing the privacy–utility trade-off with human verification in the loop.

Explainable classification · RAG + vector DB · RAG evaluation · HCI-informed design · multimodal document AI · agentic orchestration · human-in-the-loop

Mirage — Whole-of-Government synthetic data platform

Mirage is the government's self-service platform for mock and synthetic data generation, used in production by 30+ agency teams. I led the build-vs-buy evaluation against vendor systems, then built the generation stack in-house — PyTorch modeling across copula, VAE, GAN, and diffusion generators, adaptive model routing and cascading, automated hyperparameter optimization, and multi-dimensional privacy–utility evaluation.

PyTorch · VAE / GAN / diffusion · model routing & cascades · HPO · AWS
